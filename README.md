# Airbnb Global Performance & Pricing Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Airbnb data across 10 international cities to identify market leaders, pricing strategies, and service quality gaps. The goal is to provide actionable insights for potential hosts and business strategists.

## 💡 Business Insights (What I Discovered)
- **Dominant Markets:** Paris and New York lead in volume, but "Superhosts" are a minority, indicating a huge gap for quality-driven growth.
- **Pricing Strategy:** Hotel rooms average **$800**, which is nearly double the price of Private rooms ($462), showing a high demand for premium/professional stays.
- **Quality Analysis:** Cities like **Mexico City** excel in accuracy and cleanliness (9.7/9.8), whereas **Hong Kong** shows room for improvement in overall value perception.

## 🔮 Predictions & Recommendations
- **Growth Prediction:** Based on the 2015-2016 peak, the market is maturing. Future revenue growth will likely come from 'Entire Place' listings rather than shared spaces.
- **Service Optimization:** Improving communication scores in cities with lower ratings could lead to a 10-15% increase in repeat bookings.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Tool:** Power BI 
- **Data Cleaning:** Power Query (handling missing values, formatting dates)
- **Visualization:** Stacked Bar Charts, Line Trends
